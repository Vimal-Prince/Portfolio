Project Summary

I designed a tent with a wooden base and an animal skin cover as part of my texturing practice. The goal was to study natural materials and how they interact visually. The wood structure provides a sturdy framework, while the stretched animal skin introduces organic patterns, color variations, and surface imperfections. This project helped me explore how texture details contribute to authenticity in primitive, hand-crafted structures.
