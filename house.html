Project Summary

I created an old house model constructed with wood and a thatch roof as part of a study focused on texturing. The design emphasizes age and rustic character, with worn wooden beams and the natural irregularity of thatch materials. This project allowed me to practice achieving realism through texture details, experimenting with surface wear, color variation, and natural imperfections to capture the authentic look of traditional architecture.
