Project Summary

The protagonist of this 2D Halloween-themed platformer is a playful fusion of a pumpkin and a cartoon ghost, designed to embody both charm and spookiness. With its round pumpkin head and whimsical ghostly form, the character strikes a perfect balance between eerie and approachable, making it an ideal fit for a lighthearted Halloween adventure. The design emphasizes expressive features, giving the character a lively personality that appeals to players of all ages.

To bring this character to life, sprite sheets have been developed with a full range of animations. The run and jump sequences highlight its agility and cartoonish bounce, while the dash and attack animations showcase its adventurous spirit and combat readiness. A dramatic death animation completes the set, adding flair and personality even in defeat. Together, these elements create a protagonist that is both visually engaging and mechanically dynamic, driving the charm and energy of the game.
