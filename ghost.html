Project Summary

The enemy is a cartoony ghost designed to complement the playful yet spooky tone of the 2D platformer. With exaggerated features and smooth, animated movements, it strikes a balance between comical and threatening. The sprite sheet includes run and attack animations, showcasing its mischievous energy as it chases and strikes at the player. Its design ensures it feels memorable, adding personality and challenge to the Halloween-themed game world.
